HealthFit  for kids

If you want kids to exercise, then you have to make it playful and fun.
Exercises can be very beneficial for kids as their body is growing and it will lead the proper development of the kid's physical health. Along with exercises playing sports can also be helpful as kids will understand the importance to be a team.
Here are a list of exercises that are playful as well as beneficial for their health

1. Running

  1. Benefits of running:-
  2. 1.Strengthenins the muscles & lungs
  3. 2.Strong immune system
  4. 3.Improved brain function
  5. 4.Increased bone density

2. Jumping

  1. Benefits of jumping:-
  2. 1.Stimulates your metabolism.
  3. 2.​Increases oxygen capacity.
  4. 3.​Improves your sense of balance.
  5. 4.​Strengthens the heart (Cardiovascular health).

3. Skipping

  1. Benefits of skipping:-
  2. 1.Improves co-ordination.
  3. 2.Improves bone density.
  4. 3.Improves breathing efficiency.
  5. 4.​Improves brain function.

4.Squats

  1. Benefits of squats:-
  2. 1.Improves body balance.
  3. 2.Strong leg muscles.
  4. 3.Healthy joints.
  5. 4.​Improves posture.

5. Crabwalk

  1. Benefits of Crabwalk:-
  2. 1.Bilateral coordination.
  3. 2.Strength.
  4. 3.Trunk stability.
  5. 5.Motor control.

6.Sports

  1. Benefits of Sports:-
  2. 1.​Self-confidence and self-esteem.
  3. 2.Discipline.
  4. 3.Social skills.
  5. 4.Patience.
  6. 5.Perseverance.
  7. 6.Accept defeat.
  8. 7.Teamwork.

Kids should exercise for atleast 60 min per day
