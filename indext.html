HealthFit  for teens

It is very important for teens to exercise as it can affect their health in the long run. Teens needs physical activity for atleast 60 minutes. They need a proper diet that affect their mental and physical health. Teens should avoid junk food asmuch as they can if they want a healthy  body and mind.
Follow physical activities should be included in their workout hours...

1. Brisk walking

Benefits of running:-

1.Strengthenins the muscles & lungs

2.Strong immune system

3.Improved brain function

4.Increased bone density

2. Push-ups


1.Improves upper body strength.

2.​Contributes to core stability.

3.​Improves sports performance.

4.​Improves posture.

3. Squats

Benefits of squats:-

1.Improves bone health.

2.Improves balance.

3.Burns fat.

4.​Improves mobility.

4.Jumping jacks

Benefits of jumping jacks:-

1.Tones body muscles.

2.Prevents diabetes.

3.Improves mobility.

4.​Improves cardiovascular health.

5. Lunges

Benefits of Lunges:-

1.​Improve balance and coordination.

2.Boosts flexibility

3.​Better spinal health.

4.​Minimizes risk of injury.

5.Promotes weight loss

6.Sports

Benefits of Sports:-

1.​Improves muscle strength.

2.Increased oxygen supply.

3.Better sleep.

4.Improves mental health

5.Maintains healthy weight.

6.Improves heart & brain health.

Teens should exercise for 60 minutes or more.
Stay hydrated during reps.
Avoid holding breath during exercising.
