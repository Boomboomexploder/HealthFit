HealthFit  for adults

Adults need at least of 150 minutes of moderate to intense physical activity for a healthy body. You should exercise at same time everyday, stay hydrated, and get enough sleep.It will help if you strech out before and after every exercise/workout as it will ease the pain if you're a beginner.
Here are some exercises/workouts that can help you to have a more healthy and fit body...

1. Plank


Benefits of plank:-

1.Improves posture.

2.Improves metabolism.

3.Builds core strength

4.Burns calories.

5.Reduces belly fat.

2. Push-ups

Benefits of push-ups:-

1.Improves upper body strength.

2.​Contributes to core stability.

3.​Improves sports performance.

4.​Improves posture.

5.Builds muscle density.

6.Promotes weight loss.

3. Squats


Benefits of squats:-

1.Improves bone health.

2.Improves balance.

3.Burns fat.

4.​Improves mobility.

5.Tones abs and legs.

6.Builds core strength.

4.Jumping jacks


Benefits of jumping jacks:-

1.Tones body muscles.

2.Prevents diabetes.

3.Improves mobility.

4.​Improves cardiovascular health.

5.Improves ​ Coordination.

6.Builds strength in legs

5. Lunges


Benefits of Lunges:-

1.​Improve balance and coordination.

2.Boosts flexibility

3.​Better spinal health.

4.​Minimizes risk of injury.

5.Promotes weight loss

6.Mountain climbers


Benefits of Mountain climbers:-

1.​Improves muscle strength.

2.Increased oxygen supply.

3.Burns calories.

4.Improves body balance.

5.Improves heart health.

6.Burns belly fat.

7.Sit-ups


Benefits of Sit-ups:-

1.Builds core strength.

2.Improved muscle mass.

3.Prevents injury  and back pain.

4.Improves posture.

8.Crunches


Benefits of Crunches:-

1.Builds core strength.

2.Tones your abs

9.Donkey Kicks


Benefits of Donkey Kicks:-

1.​Tightens and tones the glutes and core

2.Improves stability.

3.Tones the body.

4.Builds core strength.

10.Leg raises


Benefits of Leg raises:-

1.​Reduces belly fat.

2.Lower risks of back injury.

3.Increases stability  & strength.

4.Builds stronger abs.

5.Strengthens the full core region.
