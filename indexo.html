HealthFit  for Seniors

For seniors physical activity of 150 minutes per week in moderate intensity is enough e.g. 30 minutes per 5 days of the week.
It will benefit you by keeping balance of your body and keeping you healthy and fit!
These are some exercises that you should do and some which you should avoid at all cost as they are intense and may cause injuries...

1. Chair yoga


Benefits of Chair yoga:-

1.Improves muscle strength.

2.Improves mobility.

3.Increases flexibility.

4.Improves balance.

2. Resistance band workouts


Benefits of resistance band workouts:-

1.Reduces stress in your body.

2.​Strengthens the core.

4.​Improves posture, mobility, and balance.

3. Walking


Benefits of walking:-

1.You can live longer.

2.Improves mood.

3.Increases oxygen supply.

4.​Lower risks of diseses.

5.Makes bones healthy.

5.Body weight workouts


Benefits of body weight workouts :-

1.Improves fitness level.

2.Improved flexibility.

3.Increases energy in the body.

5. Dumbell strength training


Benefits of dumbell strength training:-

1.​Increased upper body strength.

2.Improved bone health.

3.​​Enhanced metabolic fitness.

4.​Better balance & stability.
